Design Explanation: The Template Method pattern is used by defining generateReport() as a final method in the abstract Report class, locking in the workflow (loadData → formatHeader → formatBody → formatFooter). Subclasses like StudentReport and CourseReport override the abstract steps to provide their own specific behavior. The overall algorithm structure never changes, only the step implementations vary. This enforces consistency across report types while supporting polymorphism through the "List<Report>" in the driver.
